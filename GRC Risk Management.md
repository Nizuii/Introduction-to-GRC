Risk Management within GRC is not a one-time event. It is a continuous cycle that never stops as long as the organization exists.
```bash
        IDENTIFY
       (What can go wrong?)
            ↓
        ASSESS
       (How bad? How likely?)
            ↓
        MITIGATE
       (What do we do about it?)
            ↓
        MONITOR
       (Is our response working?)
            ↓
      back to IDENTIFY
       (New risks emerged?)
```
